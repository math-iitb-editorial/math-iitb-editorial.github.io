Journeys Conclude, New Paths Begin: The Department Convocation 2025

Published: 2025-10-04

The Department Convocation marks a cherished milestone in the academic journeys of students, and a moment of pride and fulfilment for their families and loved ones. This year’s Degree Award Function was held at LA 001 and saw the participation of over a hundred attendees, including graduating students, faculty, and guests.

A Day of Celebration and Remembrance

The event began on a solemn note, with the gathering observing a minute of silence in memory of Prakash Chandra Nayak, who was to receive his M.Sc. degree in Mathematics posthumously.

The guest of honour for the occasion was Professor B. V. Rao, an eminent probabilist from the Chennai Mathematical Institute. Having delivered an engaging lecture on Markov Chains the previous day, Professor Rao’s presence was received with great enthusiasm by both students and faculty. Many graduating students took the opportunity to interact with him, making it one of the most memorable moments of the event.

Following the welcome address, the Departmental Teaching Award was presented to Professor Suresh Kumar in recognition of his outstanding contributions to teaching. The ceremony continued with the distribution of academic awards to the top-performing students of the graduating batches, leading up to the highlight of the afternoon, the Degree Award Function itself. Proud smiles, photographs, and emotional farewells filled the auditorium as graduates took to the stage. The programme concluded with short addresses by graduating students, a final photo session, and an informal gathering over tea and refreshments.

Reflections from the Graduating Batch

As the graduating B.S. and M.Sc. students set forth on new journeys, many shared thoughtful reflections on their time in the department. Their paths ahead include doctoral studies, teaching positions, and careers in finance, management, data science, and software engineering.

Several students spoke appreciatively of the guidance they received from professors, teaching assistants, and peers. Others expressed that certain academic and emotional challenges might have been eased through stronger institutional support. The recently introduced regular meetings between students and faculty advisors were widely regarded as a step in the right direction, helping strengthen communication and mentorship across batches.

Graduates also highlighted the growing recognition of statistics and interdisciplinary interests within the department. Some felt that additional flexibility in crediting statistics courses towards the mathematics degrees would have been beneficial. However, they acknowledged that considerable progress has already been made on this front and expressed optimism about the department’s increasing responsiveness to students’ academic aspirations.

Learning Beyond the Classroom

The graduating students shared words of advice for their juniors, encouraging them to balance academic diligence with personal well-being.

Academically, they emphasised the importance of keeping pace with courses, solving assignments and tutorials regularly, and seeking assistance from professors, teaching assistants, and friends when necessary. Many attributed their intellectual growth to the department’s rigorous academic environment and the spirit of collaboration among peers.

Equally, they encouraged juniors to participate in sports, technical clubs, and cultural events, noting that these activities contribute significantly to personal development. They advised their successors to take up positions of responsibility only when genuinely interested, ensuring that their commitments remain meaningful and fulfilling.

Looking Ahead

As this cohort of students moves on to diverse academic and professional endeavours, their time in the Department of Mathematics at IIT Bombay remains a shared experience of growth, perseverance, and discovery. For many, it was here that they not only deepened their understanding of mathematics but also developed a sense of resilience that will serve them well in the years ahead.

While the convocation marks the conclusion of one chapter, it also signifies the beginning of many others. The graduates carry forward both the lessons and the spirit of the department, defined by curiosity, persistence, and an enduring pursuit of knowledge.
